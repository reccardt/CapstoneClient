Some of the notable artists who are among Paul's tuning customers

Dena DeRose is a jazz pianist, singer, and educator who has appeared in venues around the world with some of the most renowned jazz musicians of our time.

“Jazz singer Dena DeRose has all the vocal skills needed to rise to the top level of her field…Singing beautifully, arching through fascinating alterations of melody…she is a fluent pianist with facile technique, an imaginative approach to improvisation and a jaunty sense of swing.”

-Los Angeles Times



Geoffrey Burleson has performed to wide acclaim throughout Europe and North America, and is equally active as a recitalist, concerto soloist, chamber musician and jazz performer. The New York Times has hailed his solo performances as “vibrant” and “compelling”, and has praised his “command, projection of rhapsodic qualities without loss of rhythmic vigor, and appropriate sense of spontaneity and fetching colors”.



Hank Johnson's powerful, straight-ahead jazz piano brings to mind players like Oscar Peterson and Erroll Garner, but his style has its own unique character. He performs in the New York City area, and has been heard at such notable venues as Cleopatra's Needle and the Lenox Lounge, where New York Magazine notes, “Marquee names like Hank Johnson... pack the house on weekends”.
